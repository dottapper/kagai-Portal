How to Enjoy

Enjoying Hanamachi with Respect

Before Your Visit

  • Read Etiquette and dress appropriately
  • Be on time and follow staff instructions
  • Ask before taking photos

During the Experience

  • Enjoy performances quietly and clap at the end
  • Conversations are part of hospitality
  • Alcohol is optional; enjoy moderately

Language Support

  • English support available depending on program
  • Ask organizers in advance if you need an interpreter
  • Some terms remain Japanese; check the glossary
